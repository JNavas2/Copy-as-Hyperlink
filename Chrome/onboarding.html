Copy as Hyperlink!

This extension lets you copy selected links or the current tab Title & URL as a rich hyperlink to your clipboard. Paste into any app that supports rich text editing.

To activate:

  • Context menu: Right-click and choose Copy as Hyperlink.
  • Toolbar: Pin and then click the extension icon.
  • Keyboard shortcut: Use a shortcut. You can change this in Extensions Shortcuts settings.
Privacy: No user information is collected!
