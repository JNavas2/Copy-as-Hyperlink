Copy as Hyperlink!

Copy links or the current tab as a rich hyperlink:

  • Select a link: Copies the link as a hyperlink.
  • Select text (not a link): Copies the tab as a hyperlink, followed by your selected text.
  • No selection: Copies the tab as a hyperlink.
Paste into emails, docs, and more!
